ARM AI SoC Validation

Architected and implemented validation solutions for ARM AI SoCs, focusing on complex system-on-chip (SoC) architectures. This included validating critical components such as the Network-on-Chip (NoC), High Bandwidth Memory (HBM), PCIe, and Ethernet interfaces.

  • Utilized ARM BSA/SBSA tools for robust NVMe and NIC testing.
  • Developed validation frameworks to ensure seamless integration of high-speed components in AI-driven hardware.
  • Streamlined the verification process for complex SoC architectures to accelerate time-to-market.
