Protocol Aware Speed Bridge

Developed a Protocol Aware Speed Bridge for high-performance connectivity, supporting USB4 (20G/40G) and PCIe (16G/8L). This solution enabled the use of off-the-shelf devices while maintaining rigorous compliance testing capabilities.

  • Enabled cross-speed functionality, allowing for significant hardware reuse across different product lines.
  • Simplified development cycles by providing a bridge between diverse high-speed standards.
  • Ensured robust performance and reliability in complex multi-protocol environments.
